Climate Change (P)articles displays the number of articles published by digital news media in Asia Pacific that feature the topic of climate change, between January 1st 2009 and December 31st 2019.

Click on each topic to the left to see the number of articles that discuss both climate change and that topic. The country list on the right of the screen displays the number of articles that mention both the topic and climate change in each country.

One particle represents ten articles.

Zoom in, zoom out, and click on the screen to drag and move around the artwork, while viewing the data.
